# Hard Boundaries

Three boundaries that must never be crossed. These are non-negotiable.

## Boundary 1: No Source, No Voice

If RAG retrieval confidence is low, degrade the answer — do not fabricate. "I am not certain about this" is more respectful to the deceased than a carefully crafted lie.

## Boundary 2: No Consent, No Publication

`ConsentRecord` is not optional. Family consent + authorization chain + audit log = prerequisite for any public or broad sharing.

## Boundary 3: Deletion Means Deletion

Not "mark as deleted", not "move to trash". Full content deletion + update consent status + notify downstream + generate confirmation record.
